The Hatchback:

A Hatchback is a smaller vehicle with 2 - 4 doors (depending on the model) and a boot at the back. They are light on fuel and cost effective.

The Minivan:

A Minivan is a larger vehicle with a body that sits lower to the ground, when compared to other similar vehicles. It typically has sliding or hinged rear doors and 3 rows of seats.
