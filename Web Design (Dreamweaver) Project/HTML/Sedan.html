The Sedan:

What is a Sedan?

A Sedan is the vehicle that comes to mind when you think of the word "car", it's a regular sized vehicle with 4 doors and seats 4 - 5 people.

Is a Sedan right for me?

A Sedan has a three-box body, which means it has the engine in front, the passengers in the middle and the boot at the back. It is by far the most popular type of vehicle and not without reason. Sedans are affordably every day family cars. It can comfortably sit 4 - 5 people and allows for more than enough leg room. Sedans are lighter on fuel, so you save on petrol. It has a good amount of boot space, so daily trips aren't a hassle. The Sedan has the best performance of any standard vehicle. So if all of this sounds good to you, then maybe the Sedan is right for you!
