The Pick-Up Truck:

What is a Pick-Up Truck?

A Pick-Up truck is a much larger vehicle that has a much larger, seperate storage area.

Is a Pick-Up Truck right for me?

A Pick-Up truck is much larger than most other other vehicles and also has a much larger carrying capacity. While other vehicles may store things in the boot, a Pick-Up truck has a completely seperate area (known as the bed), at the back of the vehicle, for storing things. The maximum carrying capacity for a Pick-Up truck is 800 - 900kg. Depending on the model, a Pick-Up truck can seat 2 to 5 people. The Pick-Up truck is able to easily handle offroad traveling and the bed is great for transporting large or heavy cargo. So if this all sounds good to you, then maybe the Pick-Up truck is for you!
