The Minivan:

What is a Minivan?

A Minivan is a larger vehicle with a body that sits lower to the ground, when compared to other similar vehicles. It typically has sliding or hinged rear doors and 3 rows of seats.

Is a Minivan right for me?

A Minivan is an excellent choice for people with big families. It has more than enough headspace and sliding or hinged rear doors, so getting in and out is simple for all of your passengers. With 3 rows of seats, a minivan has a carrying capacity of 8 - 9 passengers. If the need arises for more boot space, then the last row can easily be stowed away to make more room. A benefit of the sliding rear door model is that even in tight spaces, passengers can easily enter or exit the vehicle. So if this all sounds good to you, then maybe the Minivan is right for you!
