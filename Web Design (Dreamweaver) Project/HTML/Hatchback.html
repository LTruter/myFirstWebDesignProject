The Hatchback:

What is a Hatchback?

A Hatchback is a smaller vehicle with 2 - 4 doors (depending on the model) and a boot at the back. They are light on fuel and cost effective.

Is a Hatchback right for me?

Hatchbacks are perfect for a first time car owner on a budget. They are easy to drive making them great for getting around town and since they are light on fuel, you save money on petrol. Hatchbacks (since they are on the smaller side) can fit 4 people with the maximum being 5, but this may not be very comfortable for longer trips. The main draw of Hatchbacks is that they have a stylish sleek look and are generally reliable and low maintenance. So if this all sounds good to you, then maybe the Hatchback is the right car for you!
